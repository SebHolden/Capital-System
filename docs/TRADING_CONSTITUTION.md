# Capital System — Trading Constitution

1. Losses are part of the system.
2. No untested strategy can go live.
3. Risk comes before returns.
4. No tactical trade without an exit rule.
5. Every trade needs a realistic objective.
6. Emotions must not decide.
7. The plan comes before the order.
8. Cut losses and let valid profits run.
9. Trading is treated as a business.
10. Journal is mandatory.

## Final rule

Capital System can trade only if:

* data is reliable;
* strategy is approved;
* risk is under control;
* order respects the plan;
* journal or automatic rationale exists;
* kill switch is off;
* operation is fully auditable.

If one condition fails, block the order.
